These Animals Can Survive Long After Humans Die Off

     So there was an article today in the Toronto Star about tardigrades that I thought I should share. If you didn't know, tardigrades are my favourite animal, and they are unique because they can survive nearly anything. Not many people know what they are but they can survive extreme temperatures, pressures, radiation, and even dehydration. In 2007 a study was conducted where tardigrades were sent to low Earth orbit and survived 12 days facing the vacuum, UV radiation, and cosmic rays of space. So I think a round of applause is well deserved by the microscopic water bears.

    I just want to say how proud I am that the tardigrade is featured in Star Trek Discovery, the newest series. It does make me sad, however, that they named him Ripper. Sure, there were a few misunderstandings in the beginning, but after getting to know him, the Discovery crew realises that this tardigrade is such a gentle soul. Perhaps he should be named Reginald.
